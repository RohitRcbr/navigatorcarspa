Frequently Asked Questions

What is car detailing?

Car detailing is a comprehensive cleaning and restoration process designed to maintain your vehicle’s appearance. It includes exterior cleaning (washing, waxing, and polishing) and interior cleaning (vacuuming, upholstery cleaning, and surface protection).

How often should I get my car detailed?

We recommend detailing your car every 3 to 6 months, depending on how frequently you use your car and the environmental conditions it’s exposed to. Frequent detailing helps protect your vehicle’s paint and maintain its value.

What services are included in a car detailing package?

Our detailing packages typically include exterior washing, paint protection, waxing, tire cleaning, interior vacuuming, upholstery cleaning, and dashboard conditioning. We also offer additional services like paint correction, ceramic coatings, and deep cleaning for pet hair or stains.

How long does a car detailing session take?

The time required for detailing depends on the package you choose and the condition of your vehicle. On average, a full detailing service takes between 2 to 5 hours.
